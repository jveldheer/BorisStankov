KEY TRAINING TERMS

  • RPE: Rate of Perceived Exertion. RPE 7 = 3 reps left. RPE 9 = 1 rep left
  • Tempo 3-1-2: 3 seconds down, 1 second pause, 2 seconds up
  • CNS: Explosiveness wiring - your nervous system
  • Volume: Sets × Reps × Weight

AUTO-ADJUST RULES

  • Sleep < 8.5 hr or stress 5: Cut volume 20%, remove jumps, add nap
  • New pain: Remove trigger. Do mid-range isometric 30-45s pain-free
  • Weight swings >1.5 kg overnight: Add fluids + sodium and recheck
  • Feet hot: Swap any running for bike or sled only
